使用hugo搭建个人博客并托管到github时遇到的问题&总结

在开始前我对hugo和GitHub一窍不通，只有老师给的一份说明文档。按照说明文档大致上可以完成，但其中遇到的许多问题没有那么容易就找到了解决办法，在我成功搭建博客后的第一篇文章就来记录下我遇到的问题的解决办法以及对于本次搭建博客的总结，以便于以后我继续写博客寻找资料。此文章随时更新。　总结 　首先是对于hugo的解释：　 　Hugo is a fast and modern static site generator written in Go, and designed to make website creation fun again.　　Hugo is a general-purpose website framework. Technically speaking, Hugo is a static site generator. Unlike systems that dynamically build a page with each visitor request, Hugo builds pages when you create or update your content. Since websites are viewed far more often than they are edited, Hugo is designed to provide an optimal viewing experience for your website’s end users and an ideal writing experience for website authors.
